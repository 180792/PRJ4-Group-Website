Let us tell you about ourselves...

We are a group of international students who live in the Netherlands and hope to create an experience that will simplify the world of dating for our clients.
We know that sometimes the dating field can be difficult to navigate, especially if you're a career-driven adult, a full-time parent or anything in between. You may feel like giving up, because it is so difficult to find someone you truly connect with.

Mingle wants to take all the effort of dating off your hands. We will take care of everything. Our team includes professional psychologists and dating advisors that match the couples based on the personal quiz they take when booking. After that our couples are sent on an all-inclusive trip, where their only focus will be exploring, having fun and falling in love.
Take a chance on us.
