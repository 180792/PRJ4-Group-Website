Known for its white sandy beaches, crystal clear waters, and great snorkeling places, Sicily long captivated the attention of readers, travelers, and explorers.

Sicily, or the “stone” by Italy’s “boot,” has Greek and Roman heritage, seemingly eternal sunshine and a warm sea even in November. Which makes it a perfect destination for a relaxed meditareaninen holiday.


We offer a mesmerizing 7 day experience in Sicily, with stays at the 5 star Belmond Grand Hotel Timeo in Palermo and the 5 star Sikelia Luxury Hotel in Catania. Mingles has selected for you the best spots to visit and our package includes day trips to historical sites such as Agrigento Valley of the Temples, the Roman theatre of Taorminaor the Baroque cities of Noto, Ragusa and Modica.


The island of Sicily also possesses amazing natural riches with its volcanoes Etna, Stromboli and Vulcano, its small islands, its beaches and its magnificent nature reserve in Zingaro. And fear not, we also included them in our Mingle experience.


But there is one more treasure that makes Sicily one of our, and soon one of your favorite italian destinations: well of course, the fabulous sicilian cuisine! And we have ensured that you and your partner will experience it at its fullest.
