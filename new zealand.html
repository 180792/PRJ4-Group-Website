From the famous flightless kiwi bird to the world's only alpine parrot the kea, New Zealand gives you a unique opportunity to see some intriguing birds.

From glaciers to beaches, whale-watching to wine-tasting, New Zealand has so much to offer. Not sure where to begin? Mingle has you covered.


Experience the majestic kiwi island in a 6 days all inclusive holiday package filled with action and exciting trips. Your journey, in the most southern part of the world, will start at Waiheke Island, home to a magical blend of vineyards, olive groves, beaches, art studios and funky cafes, or in other words, an enchanting oasis.


Sip New Zealand’s finest wines at one of the many vineyards while enjoying sea views. You will sleep under the star lights in rooms with retractable glass ceilings courtesy of the deluxe Five-Star accommodation at Waiheke Waterfront Lodge. The hotel is a short 35-minute ferry ride from downtown Auckland, and Mingle has planned daily activities to ensure you have the experience of a lifetime. Jet boat rides, skiing, river rafting, hiking and biking, or go bungee jumping, there is something for everyone.


And when it's time to relax, soak up Māori culture and hospitality in Rotorua, the heart of New Zealand. Visit an authentic pre-European Māori village for a cultural show. After a delicious ‘hāngī’ feast, the thermal hot pools will be calling.

Yes, it sounds too good to be true, but it isn’t. You can experience all of this in our Mingle package, price at 2200 euros.
